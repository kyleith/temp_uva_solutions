457 - Linear Cellular Automata
https://onlinejudge.org/index.php?option=com_onlinejudge&Itemid=8&page=show_problem&problem=398

The program is a cellular automata.
Each mutation cycle of a cell is based on the neighborhood density from the DNA (the density is the index).
The neighborhood density is a sum of densities of the left, current and right cells.
