340 - Master-Mind Hints
https://onlinejudge.org/index.php?option=com_onlinejudge&Itemid=8&category=98&page=show_problem&problem=276

The task is calculate "strong" and "weak" matches of two arrays.
"Strong" match means that (secretCode[i] == guessCode[i]).
"Weak" match means that the guess digit can be picked in the secret code (except other strong and weak matches) on a different index.
