10025 - The ? 1 ? 2 ? ... ? n = k problem
https://onlinejudge.org/index.php?option=com_onlinejudge&Itemid=8&category=99&page=show_problem&problem=966

There is an equation:
? 1 ? 2 ? ... ? n = k
where '?' can be '+' or '-'.

Given k value, the smallest n must be found which solved the equation.
