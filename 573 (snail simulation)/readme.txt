573 - The Snail
https://onlinejudge.org/index.php?option=com_onlinejudge&Itemid=8&page=show_problem&problem=514

The task is to simulate snail climbing from the well during the daytime, and sliding down during night.
The input is H (height), U (initial up distance), D (sliding down distance) and F (fatigue factor in %).
The output is the day when the snail has succeded or given up.
