10785 - The Mad Numerologist
https://onlinejudge.org/index.php?option=com_onlinejudge&Itemid=8&category=98&page=show_problem&problem=1726

Given the rules of building Numerologist's name based on letters' values and vowels/consonants alternation.
The vowels are on the odd positions, the consonants are on the even position.

The task is to generate name of a fixed length:
1) with the smallest sum of letters' values;
2) lexicographically smallest.
